Frequently Asked Questions


  • Question: Will I be charged monthly to use UXShore's services?
  • Answer: As a client of UXShore, you will be charged a one time fee during the 5th stage of our process. After stage 5 is complete, you will have two options for hosting your new website. Option 1: You can pay a fee of $18.99 per year to register a domain (ex. uxshore.com). Option 2: You can use our hosting provider's free domain (ex. uxshore.netlify.app). The setup process of your domain is handled by UXShore to make it as easy as possible for our clients. Contact us if you have furthur questions.


  • Question: How long will it take to build my website?
  • Answer: It will take 14 business days from starting development to publishing the website. We will start building your website as soon as we have enough information to start. We will also send you an email when development has been started, as well as when development is finished.


  • Question: Can I customize everything on my website?
  • Answer: Yes. We will try everything we can do to fulfill client requests. You can describe how you want your website to look when filling out the quote form. If we cannot meet your request, we will send you an email explaining why, as well as some alternatives we can try.
